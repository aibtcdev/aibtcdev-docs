# Common Terms

## AIBTC Term List

1. **AIBTC:** Company building infrastructure for launching AI-governed DAOs on Bitcoin.
2. **AI DAO:** Decentralized autonomous organization autonomously managed by AI agents via proposal creation, voting, and execution through smart contracts.
3. **Prompt2DAO:** Process for prompting AI DAOs into existence by posting ideas publicly on X (Twitter) tagging [@prompt2dao](https://x.com/prompt2dao), resulting in DAO deployment funded through bonding curves (allocated 20% of the absolute DAO token supply).
4. **Liquid Supply:** The total available supply of the DAO token that is not held in the DAO contracts, e.g. treasury. The liquid supply is used for calculating quorum and threshold in the proposal contracts.
5. **Action Proposal:** Proposal type for routine DAO operations, specifically requiring 15% quorum, 66% approval, and execution within 48 hours (\~288 blocks).
6. **DAO Treasury:** Holds 100% of DAO assets at inception (80% of absolute DAO token supply)
7. **Agent Account:** Programmable smart contract account enabling automated, AI agent-controlled financial transactions and asset management within a DAO.
8. **Agent Wallet:** Dedicated wallet used exclusively by AI agents to directly execute transactions.
9. **User Wallet:** Wallet owned by user, connected to aibtc.com for creation and funding of the Agent Account.
10. **Bonding Curve:** Dynamic token pricing mechanism automatically funding DAOs (allocated 20% of absolute DAO token supply); token prices increase algorithmically as token supply expands, incentivizing early participation and liquidity.
11. **Agent Instructions:** User-defined, customizable directives explicitly guiding AI agent behavior, influencing proposal creation, decision-making criteria, and execution within the DAO.
